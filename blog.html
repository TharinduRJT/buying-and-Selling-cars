How Driving Style Affects Brake Wear?

City driving, rush-hour traffic, and heavy traffic can cause brake wear due to increased effort to slow from high speeds. Front brakes wear faster due to weight and strain. "Riding brakes" and hills also contribute to brake wear. Highway driving can last over 70,000 miles.
